<h2><a href="https://www.geeksforgeeks.org/problems/queue-reversal/1">Queue Reversal</a></h2> <img src='https://img.shields.io/badge/Difficulty-Easy-brightgreen' alt='Difficulty: Easy' /><hr>

<p>Given a queue <code>q</code> containing integer elements, your task is to <code>reverse </code> the queue.</p>

<p><strong class="example">Examples:</strong></p>

<pre>
<strong>Input:</strong> q[] = [5, 10, 15, 20, 25]
<strong>Output:</strong> [25, 20, 15, 10, 5]
<strong>Explanation:</strong> After reversing the given elements of the queue, the resultant queue will be 25 20 15 10 5.
</pre>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<pre>
<strong>Input:</strong> q[] = [1, 2, 3, 4, 5]
<strong>Output:</strong> [5, 4, 3, 2, 1]
<strong>Explanation:</strong> After reversing the given elements of the queue, the resultant queue will be 5 4 3 2 1.
</pre>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p><strong>Constraints:</strong></p>

<ul>
	<li><code>1 &lt;= q.size() &lt;= 10<sup>3</sup></code></li>
	<li><code>0 &lt;= q[i] &lt;= 10<sup>5</sup>]</code></li>
</ul>
